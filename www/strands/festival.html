Turnip Throwing Contest

Ever wondered how far you can throw a turnip??
Well this is your chance to find out.
Choose from a selection of turnips and chuck one as far as you can. The person who throws the turnip the furthest is the winner.
The current record throw for this event, and also the world record, is 2 miles. If you think you can beat this then come along and test yourself.
First prize is a (wait for it) turnip.

Catch The Turnip

A crowd favourite over the past number of years. Thousands of people will line up to try and catch the Giant Turnip.
Winner for the past 2 years runnung, Michelle Forristal (pictured below) has decided to don the suit and act as this years Turnip.
Contestants have one hour to try and catch the Turnip. You can use any means necessary to get the upper hand on your opponents in the chase. The only rule is you cannot eat the Turnip (as it is only a man dressed up as one).
If somebody is lucky enough to catch the Turnip, they will walk away with the amazing prize of......A Turnip!!
